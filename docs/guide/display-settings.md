# Display Settings

Configure the visual appearance of IconChanger in **Settings** > **Display**.

## Appearance

Choose between **System**, **Light**, or **Dark** mode. The system option follows your macOS appearance setting.

### Custom Icon Badge

Toggle **Show Custom Icon Badge** to display a green checkmark on apps in the sidebar that have custom icons applied.

## Dock Preview

The Dock Preview shows a live preview of how your customized icons look in the macOS Dock.

### Preview Mode

- **Only When Dock Filter** — shows the Dock Preview only when you filter by Dock apps
- **Always** — always shows the Dock Preview at the top of the detail area

### Wallpaper Background

Toggle **Show Wallpaper Background** to display your desktop wallpaper behind the Dock Preview, matching the real Dock appearance.

When enabled, two additional sliders appear:

- **Wallpaper Spread** (50–120) — controls how far the wallpaper extends beyond the Dock area
- **Wallpaper Blur** (28–40) — controls the blur intensity of the wallpaper behind the Dock

### Dock Glass Intensity (macOS 26+)

On macOS Tahoe and later, a **Dock Glass Intensity** slider (0–1) controls the translucency of the Liquid Glass Dock frame in the preview.

## Language

Choose from 30 supported languages. Changes take full effect after restarting the app.
